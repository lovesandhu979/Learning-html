Allo Gobi

Aloo gobi is a classic vegetarian indian dish translating to potatoes cauliflower. It is a one pot,super simple,comforting dish that is ready quickly.

some variations include onion or tomatoe, which can easily be added here.

if you are looking for even more easy indian dishes, this chicken curry should be next on your list.

Either way, this dish is full of flavour and you will be making it every night.

The cost of this dish in canada is just £10.

Ingredients

  • 2tbsp.vegetable oil
  • red chill and diced
  • garam masala
  • dried turmericc
  • salt

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium high heat ,heat oil.
  2. Add chili,garlic,ginger and cook until toasted .
  3. Add all the material and reduce heat and cook ,covered,until potatoes are tender.
  4. Garnish with cilantro to serve.
